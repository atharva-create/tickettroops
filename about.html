ABOUT TICKET TROOPS

Ticket Troops is a premium experiential travel and events company, crafting bespoke global experiences that connect brands, teams, leaders, and fans through sports, concerts, and entertainment.

Our Story

Ticket Troops was born from a personal frustration we all know too well - the struggle of accessing premium live events. Long queues, exorbitant prices, unreliable sources, and worst of all, missing out on our dream events. We decided it was time to change that.

What started as a passion for sports evolved into something bigger. With a decade of experience in the hospitality industry and armed with expertise from the best business schools, we transformed our vision into reality - making premium experiential travel accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Crafting Unforgettable Live Experiences - We're dedicated to transforming global events into top-notch experiential travel moments, making premium access available to all.

Our Vision

To be your gateway to live global experiences, building the TT ARMY - a community of event enthusiasts united by unforgettable moments.
