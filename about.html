ABOUT TICKET TROOPS

Our Journey

Passion Into Profession

With a passion for sports and a decade of experience in hospitality, our founders turned their love for live events into the art of curating luxury experiences.

Founding The Brand

Built on strong global partnerships and a deep understanding of clients' needs, Ticket Troops was founded to make premium experiential travel accessible to all.

Growth & Excellence
